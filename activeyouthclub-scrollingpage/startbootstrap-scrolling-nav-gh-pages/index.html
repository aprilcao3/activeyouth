Our Mission

Active Youth Badminton Club is an organization founded and run by youth. Our mission is to promote the growth and development of badminton by providing high quality, low cost coaching to players of all levels. Through our badminton lessons and camps, we create a positive and supportive learning environment where players are encouraged to improve their skills, build confidence, and cultivate a passion for the sport. We are committed to fostering a sense of community among players and coaches, and to promoting sportsmanship, teamwork, and respect both on and off court. Our ultimate goal is to inspire a lifelong love of badminton and to help players reach their full potential.
